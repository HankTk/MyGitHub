Vessel Card Area

The vessel card area also known as vessel carousel appears below the Search field of your SMARTShip system. The various vessels which are under your company as well as assigned to you appears here as cards.

Each vessel card represents each vessel. The following information appears in the vessel card:

  • Vessel Name - Indicates the name of the vessel.

  • Last Data Received - Indicates the online/offline status of the vessel's SMARTShip system with the time when the last data was received. The online/offline status is indicated by the following colored indicators:

    • - Online status

    • - Offline status

  • ME RPM - Indicates the operating speed of the vessel engine.

  • Vessel speed - Indicates the speed of the vessel.

  • Vessel Status - The following can be the status of the vessel:

    • At Anchor - If the status is 'At Anchor', the green arrow at the bottom of the vessel card  is placed till the end of the line.

    • Underway using Engine - If the status is 'Underway using Engine', the green arrow is placed at middle of the line indicating that the vessel is in transit.

  • Starting Point

  • Destination

  • Actual Time of Departure (ATD)

  • Estimated Time of Arrival (ETA)

If there are multiple vessels and you want to slide to the next set of vessels in the vessel card area, click the arrow button

If you want to hide the Vessel Card area from the SMARTShip dashboard, click My Fleets. To unhide, click My Fleets again
