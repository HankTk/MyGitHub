Various features of Vessel View

The appearance of the vessel view remains the same as that of the dashboard view. However, the components of the dashboard are replaced with vessel specific details.

The following features appear in the vessel view:

  • Vessel Card - Appears on the top-left corner of the vessel view. It displays the details of the vessel as mentioned in Vessel Card Area.

  • Live video of assets - This appears to the right of the vessel card. It provides a streaming live video of the various assets of the vessel. Each asset video appears in separate cards.

    1. Click Play in the video card. The video starts playing.

Once the video starts playing, you can also get a maximized view of the video in the vessel . To get this:

  1. Click . The maximized view of the video appears in the pane below.

  • Alert Area - The alert area appears below the vessel card. Here, the alert area shows alerts specific the selected vessel only.

You can do all the actions corresponding to an alert from here. For more information on alerts, refer to Alert Area.

  • Color Coded Dials - The color coded dials in the map of the vessel view shows the alerts specific to the selected vessel. Each color represents the criticality of the alert.

  • Assets - The asset area of the vessel view shows the various assets corresponding to the vessel.
