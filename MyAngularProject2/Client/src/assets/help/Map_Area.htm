Map Area

The zoomable world map area in the SMARTShip dashboard shows the vessels assigned to you as markers. The markers are placed based on their current location. The following table describes the various color coded markers used to indicate the vessels:

Marker color

Description

Green

Indicates vessels with no alerts

Red

Indicates vessels with critical, high, medium or low level alerts

Orange

Indicates vessels with high, medium or low level alerts

Yellow

Indicates vessels with medium or low level alerts

Blue

Indicates vessels with low level alerts only

The four color coded dials in the map gives the total as well as the separate count of all the alert types of your assigned vessels.
