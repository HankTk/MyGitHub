Change Profile Settings

To change profile settings:

  1. Click Profile. Your SMARTShip profile details appears:

  1. Click Edit . The following area appears which allows you to edit your profile details:

  1. You can edit the following details :

  • Name - Enter your SMARTShip user name.

  • Email - Enter the required E-mail address to which you want associate your SMARTShip account.

  • Update password - Select, if you want to update your SMARTShip password. Once clicked, the following are appears:

    • Enter your new password, confirm and click UPDATE.

  • Phone No - Enter the phone number to which you want associate your SMARTShip account.

  • Security Question - Select the required security question, if you are unable to log on to your SMARTShip account.

  • Answer - Enter the required answer for the selected security question.

  1. Once the required details are updated, click UPDATE. Your profile details are now updated.
