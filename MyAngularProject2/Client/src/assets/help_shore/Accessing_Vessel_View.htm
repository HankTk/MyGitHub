Accessing Vessel View

Vessel view gives a detailed outlook of an individual vessel. You can also view the details of the various assets, devices, alerts, reports and cases related to the corresponding vessel from here.

There are multiple ways in which you can access the vessel view. To do this:

  • From the SMARTShip dashboard, click the required vessel card from the vessel card area.

Or

  • Search and select the vessel from the search field on top of the SMARTShip dashboard.

Or

  • From the SMARTShip dashboard, hover over to the map area and click the required vessel marker.

The markers in the map view are replaced and the vessel view for the corresponding vessel appears as shown in the below image:

Hovering over the vessel marker shows other details specific to the vessel in a card format.

The following details appear in the card:

  • Latitude

  • Longitude

  • Ship Speed

  • Engine Speed

  • Wind Speed

  • DRAFT:AFT

  • DRAFT:FORE
