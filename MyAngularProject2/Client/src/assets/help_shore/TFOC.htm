TFOC

Total Fuel Oil Consumption (TFOC) is a next generation voyage optimization application which is integrated into the SMARTShip system. It provides the total fuel and oil consumption for your vessel's voyages. It also uses various methods to provides voyage route recommendations based on the collected data.

Using the following features of TFOC, you are able to achieve the best fuel and voyage optimization:

  • Provides the fuel consumptions for all the past and current voyages and forecasts for the future voyages of your vessel.

  • Provides the predicted and reported routes for a voyages. TFOC provides a forecast or recommended route for the vessel in order to make the maximum fuel savings.

  • Provides other related data such as fuel used, distance travelled, carbon emission savings and daily savings.

  • Recommends multiple options to reach your destination by proposing speeds to delay or skip a bad weather situation. It also recommends voyage plans to reach the destination at the shortest possible time (fastest).
