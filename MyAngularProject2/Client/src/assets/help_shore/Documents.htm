Documents

This tab allows you to view and download documents related to the asset. These documents could be user manual, troubleshooting guides, Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) manual and so on associated to the asset or sub-system. These are uploaded by your SMARTShip administrator.

The documents attached to the Diagnostics tab requires the corresponding application to be installed in your computer in order to open them. Example: A word document requires Microsoft Word, a PDF document requires Adobe Acrobat® reader, and a spreadsheet requires Microsoft Excel® and so on.
