Ship Assets View

Overview

The Ship Asset View feature from the Vessel View allows you to view the details of the various assets associated with your vessel. It allows you to view, monitor and analyse the various performance parameters of the equipment or assets in the vessel and plan your vessel operation accordingly.

In the asset view, the different assets of your vessel are divided into three different categories. They are:

  • Engine Room Machinery

  • Hull and Cargo

  • Navigation Equipments

Each asset category lists the various systems and subsystems it contains. A system could be a single equipment or group of equipment. A subsystem could be a single sensor or a group of sensors from these equipment.

Example: The Navigation Systems category has AIS as system and subsystems as AI SSD and AI VSD

Each subsystem upon selection, displays the list of sensors associated with the system, the status and the value of the data incoming from the sensors. Using this data, you can monitor and analyse the performance of your assets.

Accessing Ship Assets View

To access Ship Assets View:

  1. From the Vessel View of the corresponding vessel, hover your mouse pointer over Ship Assets View. An enlarged pane appears which allows you to select the asset category or the My Favorites tab in Ship Assets View.
