Various Tabs of Ship Assets View

The different tabs of your Ship Assets View are:

  • Engine Room Machinery

  • Hull and Cargo

  • Navigation Equipments

  • My Favorites

Engine Room Machinery

Displays the assets or subsystems which comes under the Engine Room Machinery category of your vessel such as air and ventilation, boiler system, AMS, air handling unit and so on. Upon selection, the system window appears:

The above image shows the system Main Engine which comes under Engine and Machinery.

The tabs of the subsystem are common across all the subsystems belonging to all categories. For more detailed information on this, refer to Various Tabs of a Subsystem.

Hull and Cargo

Displays the assets or subsystems which comes under the Hull and Cargo assets category of your vessel such as gas detection system, N2 Generator and so on.

Navigation Equipment

Displays the assets or subsystems which comes under the navigation systems assets category of your vessel such as Anemometer, Magnetic Compass, GPS and so on.

My Favorites

This tab allows you to access the favorites you have set from the Diagnostics section of your SMARTShip system for easy access.

Selecting a favorite under My Favorite Charts  takes you to RMD area of your SMARTShip system and displays the plotted chart.

For detailed information on RMD, refer here.

If you have not created any favorites from RMD, the area appears empty

Using the Search field, you can select the required RMD favorite from here.

You can also sort the favorites in alphabetical order using the sort button next to the search field. To remove any favorite from here, click the delete button beside the favorite.
