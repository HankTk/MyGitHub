Case Management Overview

Case Management enables you to centralize, manage, and process information about a complex problem or issue. This information is captured in a centralized point known as case. The case contains the various resolutions and updated information regarding the issue or problem.

The case management feature of SMARTShip system allows you create cases based on alerts or demand. This allows you to have an alert based conversation or discussion thread.

  • From the Apps section of vessel view, select Cases . The Case Management area appears below:

The number against the case icon indicates the number of notifications for the case management system.
