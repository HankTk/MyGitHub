Role Management

Role Management allows you to create various roles for your SMARTShip system. You can create a role and also decide the areas of the SMARTShip system the role has access.

Example: You can create a role called 'Additional Chief Officer' and assign access to RMD, Reports and Case Management.

This custom role can be assigned to any user using User Management.

To access Role Management:

  • Click Settings and select Role Management from the menu. The Role Management window appears:

 

The Role Management list pane shows the following details:

  • Role - Displays the name of the role. The following are the default roles in the SMARTShip system.

    • Admin   

    • Captain

    • Chief Engineer

    • Engineer

    • Fleet Manager

    • Shore Staff

    • Technical Superintendent

  • Company - Displays the company to which the role belongs.
