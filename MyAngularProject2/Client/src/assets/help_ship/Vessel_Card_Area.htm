Vessel Card Area

The vessel card area also known as vessel carousel appears on top of your SMARTShip system. The various vessels which are under your company as well as assigned to you appear here in a card format.

Each vessel card represents a separate vessel. The following information appears in each vessel card:

  • Vessel Name - Indicates the name of the vessel.

  • Last Data Received - Indicates the online/offline status of the vessel's SMARTShip system with the time received from the vessel. The online/offline status is indicated by the following colored indicators:

    • - Online status

    • - Offline status

  • ME RPM - Indicates the operating speed of the vessel engine.

  • Vessel speed - Indicates the speed of the vessel.

  • Vessel Status - The following can be the status of the vessel:

    • At Anchor - If the status is 'At Anchor',there is a blue line indicating the vessel is at anchor.

    • Underway using Engine - If the status is 'Underway using Engine', there is a  green arrow which indicates the progress of the voyage.

  • Actual Time of Departure (ATD)

  • Estimated Time of Arrival (ETA)

  • Starting Point

  • Destination

Clicking on the vessel card takes you to the vessel view of the corresponding vessel.
