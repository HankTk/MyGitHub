Getting Started with SMARTShip

Now that you have familiarized yourself with the overview of SMARTShip and its features, it is time for you get started with SMARTShip and start using it. It is recommended that you follow the steps in the order given below for the correct setting up and functioning of your SMARTShip system.

Step 1: Log onto SMARTShip

  1. Open your web browser and in the address bar, enter the following URL:

https://smartship.alphaorimarine.com/

The below login window appears:

Follow the steps as mentioned in Login and Access and the SMARTShip vessel view appears.

Step 2: Start Using the Vessel View

The SMARTShip Vessel View gives a detailed overview of your assigned vessel. You are able to access the various alerts, reports, apps and assets specific to your vessel.

  • For detailed information on vessel view, refer to Accessing Vessel View.

  • For detailed information on the various Apps in vessel view such as Cases, RMD, Reports, TFOC and Chat, refer to Apps in Vessel View.

  • For detailed information on the various assets and subsystems of your vessel, refer to Ship Assets View.

While referring to the additional topics for detailed information, please make sure to refer the Related Topics as well for better understanding of all the related features

Step 3: Set Up Your Profile Details

Now that you have familiarized yourself with SMARTShip vessel view and its features, you may want to change your profile details such as your personal details, communication addresses such as Email and Telephone number. All SMARTShip communications and notifications are sent to the Email address and Telephone number mentioned in your My Profile settings.

    • To set up your profile details, refer to Profile Settings.

Step 4: Create and Subscribe to Alerts

Once you have updated your profile details,you may want to create and subscribe additional alerts apart from the various alerts already present in your Alert Area in Dashboard.

  • To create and manage alerts, refer to Alert Management.

  • To subscribe to alerts, refer to Alert Subscription.

Step 5: Configure and Subscribe Reports

Now that you have created and subscribed alerts, you may want to configure and subscribe additional reports in your SMARTShip system as well. These reports can be configured based on different in-built templates.

  • To configure reports, refer to Report Configuration.

Additional Steps

  • For detailed information on how to create and manage roles, refer to Role Management.

  • For detailed information on how to create and manage users and assign roles, refer to User Management.
