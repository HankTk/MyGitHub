Overview

SMARTShip is an Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT) based system capable of serving multiple vessels of your company. It connects all the equipment on-board the vessel internally as well as to the Internet. This makes the data on every single component available to the SMARTShip user which in turn allows remote monitoring, diagnostics and control, decision support systems, real-time analytics, maintenance and so on.

How it Works?

The SMARTShip system collects data from the various assets or components of your vessel using data acquisition units such as sensors or actuator modules installed on-board the vessel. This acquired data is used by the SMARTShip application which acts as a central console to monitor and manage connected devices, both locally and remotely. The data acquired from the data acquisition units can also be used for analysis and maintenance for increasing the overall operational efficiency and cost optimization of your vessel.

Features and Benefits

The following features of SMARTShip can be used for the smooth operation of your vessel:

  • A Dashboard which provides a detailed outlook of the vessels that are assigned to you. It also provides the various information associated with the vessels such as Alerts, Reports, Cases and so on.

  • Alert Management System for generating alerts based on the data acquired from the data acquisition units and the conditions that you provide. Alerts can be subscribed via Dashboard, Email or SMS. Example: You may want to generate an alert when the temperature in a specific asset is beyond the recommended value.

  • An extensive Vessel Asset View which gives a comprehensive overview of all the assets of your vessel. You can also view details of the data inbound from the various sub subsystems under each asset.

  • Remote Monitoring and Diagnosis (RMD) display live data fetched from sensor in a graphical format with real-time guidance to the ship master.

  • Total Fuel Oil Consumption (TFOC), a next generation voyage and fuel optimization application.

  • Reports based on pre-defined templates for analysis and better decision making.

  • Live streaming of assets for monitoring.

  • Case Management system to centralize, manage and process information about a complex problem or issue.

  • Chat application to communicate with the different SMARTShip users.

  • Role Management which allows you to create roles with access to specific features of your SMARTShip system.

  • User Management for creating and managing users. You can also assign roles to users from here.

Using the above capabilities of SMARTShip, you are able to achieve the following benefits:

  • Cost optimization by real-time monitoring of devices and on-board machinery using a centralized system

  • Increase the overall operational efficiency through proactive and corrective actions using real time data

  • Reduce risk due to gut based decision making through use of real time data analytics

  • Promote safety & greener environment by reducing near misses  on board ships

  • Better grip on compliance needs with integrated ship management  

  • Reduce injury & cargo loss claims with SMART prediction & analysis

  • Enhanced automation and get benchmarking insights from central database  to prevent losses due to human error

  • Turn ship operations into a platform play that can integrate with all other ancillary services
