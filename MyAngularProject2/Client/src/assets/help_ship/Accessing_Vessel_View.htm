Vessel View

Vessel View gives comprehensive overview of your assigned vessel. It also allows you view and access the various associated information and apps of the vessel.

The position of your vessel in the global map is indicated using a vessel marker. Hovering over the vessel marker shows other details specific to the vessel in a card format.

The following details appear in the card:

  • Latitude

  • Longitude

  • Ship Speed

  • Engine Speed

  • Wind Speed

  • DRAFT:AFT

  • DRAFT:FORE
