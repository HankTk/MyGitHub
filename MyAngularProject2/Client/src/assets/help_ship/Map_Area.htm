Map Area

The zoomable world map area in the SMARTShip vessel view shows the vessels assigned to you in the form of markers as highlighted in the image below. The markers are placed based on their current position of the vessel in the geographical map.

The following table describes the various color coded markers used to indicate vessels with alerts:

Marker color

Description

Red

Indicates vessels with critical, high, medium or low level alerts

Orange

Indicates vessels with high, medium or low level alerts

Yellow

Indicates vessels with medium or low level alerts

Blue

Indicates vessels with low level alerts only

Green

Indicates vessels with no alerts
