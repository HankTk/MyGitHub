Various features of Vessel View

The vessel view displays the details specific to the vessel as well the various associated information, features and apps corresponding to the selected vessel.

The following features appear in the vessel view:

  • Vessel Card - Appears on the top-left corner of the vessel view. It displays the details of the vessel as mentioned in the Vessel Card Area.

  • Vessel Detail Card - Appears next to the vessel card. It displays the various parameters associated with the vessel. They are:

    • Propeller slip

    • ME fuel consumption

    • DG fuel consumption

    • BLR fuel consumption

  • Live Stream of Assets and Devices - This appears to the right of the vessel card. It provides a streaming live video of the various assets of the vessel. Each asset video appears in separate cards.

    1. Click Play in the video card. The video starts playing.

Once the video starts playing, you can also get a maximized view of the video in the vessel. To get this:

  1. Click . The maximized view of the video appears in the pane below and the video stops playing in the video card.

  • Alert Pane - By default, the alert area remains hidden in the vessel view and only the alert meter remains in the vessel view. Selecting the alert meter displays the alert area with the alerts corresponding to the selected vessel.

In the vessel view, the alert area displays alerts corresponding to the vessel only. For detailed information, refer to Alert Area.

  • Ship Assets View - The asset area of the vessel view shows the various assets corresponding to the vessel. It is categorized into the various systems and subsystems of your vessel.

For detailed information on assets, refer to Ship Assets View.
